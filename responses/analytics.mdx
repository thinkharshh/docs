# Analytics

Analyze participant performance patterns across multiple sessions.

## Session Metrics

Each session captures:

- **Overall Score**: Weighted average across all goals
- **Duration**: Actual session length vs. configured maximum
- **Goal Breakdown**: Individual scores per goal
- **Speech Metrics**: Filler words, pacing, vocabulary patterns

## Interpreting Scores

### Individual Session
A single session score tells you how one participant performed against your goals. Use the detailed analysis to understand why.

### Across Sessions
Compare scores across multiple participants to:
- Identify which goals are hardest (low average scores)
- See if your scenario difficulty is appropriate
- Find patterns in where participants struggle

## Tips for Better Analytics

<Tip>
If all participants score above 90%, your goals might be too easy. If everyone scores below 60%, the scenario might be too challenging or the scoring instructions too strict. Aim for a distribution that differentiates performance levels.
</Tip>

- Review edge cases (highest and lowest scores) to validate your scoring
- Iterate on scoring instructions if results don't match your intuition
- Use the transcript to verify the AI's evaluation makes sense
