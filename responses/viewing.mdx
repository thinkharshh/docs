Every completed session produces a response with transcript, recording, goal scores, and analytics.

## Accessing Responses

Open your scenario and click the **Responses** tab. You'll see a table of all completed sessions.

## Response Table

Each row shows:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Participant | Name of who took the session |
| Score | Average percentage across all goals |
| Date | When the session was completed |
| Duration | How long the session lasted (MM:SS) |
| Last Activity | Time since completion |

## Filtering

Use the filters above the table to narrow results:

- **Search**: Find by participant name
- **Score Range**: Excellent (90%+), Good (80-89%), Average (70-79%), Fair (60-69%), Poor (\<60%)
- **Date Range**: Today, This Week, This Month, All Time

## Detailed Analysis

Click any row to open the full analysis view:

### Transcript
The complete conversation between the participant and AI persona, with timestamps.

### Goal Scores
Individual score and written feedback for each goal. See exactly where the participant excelled or fell short.

### Strengths & Growth Areas
AI-generated summary of what went well and what to improve.

### Speech Analysis
- Filler words count and frequency
- Weak words and hedging language
- Sentence starters and patterns

### Recording Playback
Play back the full session audio/video recording.

## Export

Click the **Export** button to download all filtered results as an Excel spreadsheet. Includes participant names, scores, dates, and durations.

<Info>
Responses update in real-time. If you see "Analysis pending" with a spinner, the AI is still processing -- results typically appear within 1-2 minutes after session end.
</Info>
