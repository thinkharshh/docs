# How to Embed a Scenario on Your Website

Add an AI conversation directly to your website -- no redirect needed.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Share modal">
    Open your scenario and click **Share** in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Embed tab">
    Click the **"Embed"** tab in the modal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your embed format">
    Select one:
    - **Avatar Assist** (176x265px): A floating avatar widget in the corner of your page. Visitors click to start chatting.
    - **Inline** (800x600px default): Full meeting UI embedded in your page content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure dimensions (Inline only)">
    For Inline embeds, adjust:
    - **Width**: Drag or type a value (300-2000px)
    - **Height**: Drag or type a value (400-1200px)
    - Check **Fullscreen** to let participants expand
    - Check **Responsive** to scale with container width
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the embed code">
    Click **"Copy Embed Code"**. You'll get an iframe snippet like:
    ```html
    <iframe 
      src="https://meet.waterr.ai/embed/inline/abc123" 
      width="800px" 
      height="600px" 
      frameborder="0"
      allow="camera; microphone; autoplay; display-capture; fullscreen"
      style="max-width: 100%;">
    </iframe>
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste into your website">
    Add the iframe code to your HTML where you want the scenario to appear.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Requirements

<Warning>
Your website must use **HTTPS**. Browsers block microphone and camera access on non-secure origins.
</Warning>

## Testing Your Embed

After adding the code:
1. Load your webpage
2. The embed should show the meeting UI (or floating avatar)
3. Click to start -- grant microphone permissions when prompted
4. Verify the AI persona responds correctly

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Embed shows blank | Ensure scenario visibility is set to public |
| "Permission denied" for microphone | Switch your site to HTTPS |
| Embed too small on mobile | Enable the "Responsive" option |
| Audio not playing | Add `autoplay` to the iframe's `allow` attribute |
