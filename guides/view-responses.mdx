---
title: "How to View Responses"
description: "Step-by-step guide to reviewing session results, scores, and transcripts."
---

See how participants performed, read transcripts, and review goal scores.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your scenario">
    Navigate to the scenario you want to review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the Responses tab">
    In the scenario detail page, click **"Responses"** (the last tab).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the results table">
    You'll see all completed sessions listed with:
    - **Participant name**
    - **Score** (average % across goals)
    - **Date and time**
    - **Duration**
    - **Last activity**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter results (optional)">
    Use the controls above the table:
    - **Search**: Type a participant name
    - **Score filter**: Select a range (Excellent, Good, Average, Fair, Poor)
    - **Date filter**: Today, This Week, This Month, or All Time
  </Step>
  <Step title="View detailed analysis">
    Click any row to open the full breakdown:
    - **Transcript**: Complete conversation with timestamps
    - **Goal Scores**: Individual score + feedback for each goal
    - **Strengths**: What the participant did well
    - **Growth Areas**: Where to improve
    - **Speech Analysis**: Filler words, weak language, patterns
    - **Recording**: Play back the audio/video
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export (optional)">
    Click **Export** to download all filtered results as an Excel file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Scores

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 90-100% | Excellent -- exceeded all criteria |
| 80-89% | Good -- met most criteria |
| 70-79% | Average -- partially met criteria |
| 60-69% | Fair -- significant gaps |
| Below 60% | Poor -- did not meet criteria |

<Info>
If you see "Analysis pending" with a loading spinner, the AI is still processing the session. Results typically appear within 1-2 minutes after the session ends.
</Info>

## Tips

- **Refresh**: Click the refresh button if you're waiting for new results
- **Delete**: Hover over a row and click the delete icon to remove a response (permanent)
- **Compare**: Look at the same goal across multiple participants to spot patterns
