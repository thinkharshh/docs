Waterr AI lets you create realistic AI conversation scenarios that simulate interviews, sales calls, training sessions, and any structured interaction. Your participants talk to an AI persona that evaluates their performance in real-time.

## What you can build

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Interview Practice" icon="message-question">
    Simulate behavioral, technical, or HR interviews with AI interviewers that adapt to candidate responses.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sales Training" icon="phone">
    Practice discovery calls, objection handling, and closing with AI prospects that push back realistically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Product Onboarding" icon="box">
    Guide new users through your product with an AI that walks them through features interactively.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Support Training" icon="headset">
    Train support agents on handling difficult customers, escalations, and technical troubleshooting.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Scenario">
    Describe what you need in plain language, or start from a template. The AI generates a complete scenario with a persona, instructions, and goals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share with Participants">
    Generate a link, embed it on your website, or schedule sessions with specific people.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Results">
    After each session, the AI scores participants on your defined goals. View transcripts, recordings, and analytics.
  </Step>
</Steps>
