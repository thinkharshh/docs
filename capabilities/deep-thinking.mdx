---
title: Deep Thinking
description: "Enable extended reasoning for complex, nuanced AI conversations."
---

Deep Thinking gives your AI persona additional reasoning time to produce more thoughtful, accurate, and nuanced responses. This is especially useful for technical interviews, complex problem-solving, and scenarios that require careful analysis.

## How it works

When Deep Thinking is enabled, the AI takes a moment to reason through its response before speaking. This produces:

- **More accurate technical answers** -- Better code review, system design discussions, and analytical reasoning
- **More nuanced evaluation** -- Deeper understanding of participant responses for more precise scoring
- **Better follow-up questions** -- The AI identifies gaps and inconsistencies more effectively

## Enabling Deep Thinking

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your scenario">
    Navigate to the scenario you want to configure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Click the **Settings** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Deep Thinking">
    Toggle on **Deep Thinking** under capabilities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Deep Thinking adds a brief pause (1-3 seconds) before the AI responds. For fast-paced conversational scenarios like sales calls, you may prefer to leave it off for a more natural flow.
</Note>

## Trade-offs

| With Deep Thinking | Without Deep Thinking |
|---|---|
| More accurate, nuanced responses | Faster, more conversational flow |
| Better at complex reasoning | Better for casual, rapid-fire dialogue |
| Slightly longer response time | Near-instant responses |
| Higher quality goal scoring | Standard goal scoring |
