title: Consent
description: "Show a custom consent message before the participant joins — for recording, data use, or any disclosure your team requires."
---

If your meetings handle anything sensitive — recordings, health information, candidate evaluations, financial discussion — you can require participants to read and accept a consent message before they're allowed to join.

The consent screen appears **before** the device check, so the participant agrees before the AI ever sees them.

## Setting it up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your scenario">
    Switch to the **Settings** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable consent">
    Toggle on **Require consent**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your consent message">
    Plain text or markdown. Use markdown links to point participants at your Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, or any external page. Example:

    ```markdown
    This session is recorded and analyzed by AI for training quality.
    By continuing you agree to our [Terms](https://example.com/terms)
    and [Privacy Policy](https://example.com/privacy).
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    The next participant who tries to join sees the modal before they can hit "Start".
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the participant sees

A modal with your message and two options:

- **I understand** — accept and continue to the device check
- **Cancel** — navigate away from the meeting

If the participant cancels, they don't join the meeting and the AI never receives them.

## Use cases

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Recording Disclosure" icon="microphone">
    Disclose that the session is recorded, transcribed, and reviewed. Required in many jurisdictions for two-party-consent recording.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Candidate Screening" icon="user-tie">
    Disclose that a recruiter or hiring manager may review the AI evaluation. Get explicit agreement before the interview begins.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Healthcare Training" icon="user-doctor">
    Display HIPAA or PHI handling disclosures before clinicians enter a simulated patient encounter.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Research Studies" icon="flask">
    Capture informed-consent text for IRB-governed user research sessions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Markdown support

The consent message renders as markdown. The most useful pieces:

- `**bold**` and `*italic*` for emphasis
- `[Link text](https://example.com)` for external links — opens in a new tab
- Line breaks render as paragraph breaks

Keep the message focused. A 200-word consent block has read-through; a 2,000-word block gets dismissed.

## What's stored

The consent screen is a **pre-join gate**, not a stored agreement record. The participant's click is enforced (they can't proceed without it) but isn't logged to an auditable consent table.

If your compliance program requires an audit trail of *who* consented to *what version* of the message *when*, capture that on your own backend before redirecting the participant to the meeting URL, or contact us about extending the model.
