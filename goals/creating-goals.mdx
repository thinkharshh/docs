## Add a Goal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Goals tab">
    Navigate to your scenario and click the **Goals** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Goal">
    Click the **Add Goal** button to open the goal creation form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    - **Name**: A short label (e.g., "Active Listening")
    - **Description**: One sentence of context
    - **Scoring Instructions**: Detailed criteria for the AI evaluator
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click Save. The goal applies to all future sessions for this scenario.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Writing Scoring Instructions

Scoring instructions tell the AI evaluator exactly what to look for. The quality of your scoring instructions directly determines the quality of evaluation.

### Good vs. Bad Instructions

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Good">
    ```text
    Evaluate whether the participant:
    1. Acknowledged the customer's frustration before offering solutions
    2. Asked at least one clarifying question to understand the root cause
    3. Provided a specific, actionable resolution (not generic platitudes)
    4. Confirmed the customer's satisfaction before ending
    
    Score 90-100 if all four are met.
    Score 70-89 if three are met.
    Score 50-69 if two are met.
    Score below 50 if fewer than two are met.
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Bad">
    ```text
    Did they handle the customer well?
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Tips

- **Be specific about what "good" looks like.** Use observable behaviors, not subjective judgments.
- **Include a scoring rubric.** Define what each score range means.
- **Test with Playground.** Run a session and check if the scores make sense for your criteria.
- **Use 3-5 goals per scenario.** Too many goals dilute focus. Too few miss important dimensions.

## Example Goals by Scenario Type

### Interview Scenario
| Goal | Scoring Focus |
|------|---------------|
| Technical Depth | Did they explain concepts clearly with concrete examples? |
| Problem Solving | Did they break down the problem, consider trade-offs, and arrive at a reasonable solution? |
| Communication | Were answers structured, concise, and easy to follow? |

### Sales Scenario
| Goal | Scoring Focus |
|------|---------------|
| Discovery Quality | Did they ask open-ended questions that uncovered real pain points? |
| Objection Handling | Did they acknowledge concerns and provide evidence-based responses? |
| Next Steps | Did they propose a clear, specific next action? |
